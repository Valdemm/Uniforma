About Us

Ksimel Publishing, Inc. is one of the leading USA developers, manufacturers and suppliers of overalls and personal protective equipment. We implement complex solutions for labor protection at enterprises, provide such modern services as outsourcing of PPE, rental and washing of workwear, custom-made workwear, corporate identity development, consulting in the field of labor protection, assessment of production risks, etc.

The company began its activity with the sale of overalls for civil aviation, and this left a special imprint on our work - the exceptional demands on product quality adopted in aviation has become one of our main principles.

Since then, we have been helping to protect the life and health of people in many professions whose work is associated with occupational risks - pilots, oil workers, metallurgists, builders, miners, doctors, cooks and many others.

Occupational safety today is not only health care and protection against occupational risks, but also a means of increasing labor productivity and employee loyalty to their company.

From industrial uniforms, career wear and image apparel, to highly specialized protective clothing, casual wear and floorcare products, Ksimel Publishing, Inc.-made products stand for quality and a commitment to providing maximum comfort, durability, and safety.

Technology

1 Technologies in the production of workwear
Ksimel Publishing, Inc. has its own production base that consists of 9 sewing factories, which are equipped with modern high-performance equipment that can significantly improve the protective and operational properties of workwear and reduce the time spent on its manufacture.
2 Klopman Fabrics
Ksimel Publishing, Inc. clothes are made only from the best materials.
Quality workwear can only be made from quality fabrics. Therefore, we choose only reliable suppliers of fabrics, insulation and accessories.

Thus, our partner company Klopman is the first European company specializing in fabrics specifically for work and corporate clothing. It pioneered a 35% cotton/65% polyester blend, which has since become the most popular blend in specialty fabrics.
3 GORE-TEX Technology
Provides superior waterproof, windproof and vapor permeability (breathability) - the best combination of GORE-TEX product comfort ratings , while being one of the lightest and most bulky garments on the market.

Provides protection against short term flame exposure . When exposed to flame, the top layer melts and GORE® PYRAD® technology forms a non-combustible carbon bed (carbonizes) . The layer is continuous and securely soldered to the membrane, completely preventing the penetration of the flame for a long time.
4 Ansell Innovative Technologies
Over the years, Ansell has pioneered many innovations in gloves that have become industry standards. A hallmark of our approach to innovation is turning global insights into end-user needs into technology solutions that enhance worker comfort, efficiency and protection.
5UVEX Eye Protection Technologies
Ksimel Publishing, Inc. introduces UVEX goggles to the USA market. UVEX is one of the world 's leading manufacturers of eyewear.

80% of the entire range of uvex eyewear is manufactured in our own factory, where many years of experience in production and the latest innovations are concentrated.

UVEX goggles offer the highest level of protection with optimum comfort today. This is achieved due to the fact that a wide range of glasses takes into account all kinds of individual characteristics of people, such as the shape of the head and face, the distance between the pupils.
